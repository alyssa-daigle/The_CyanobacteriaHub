Welcome to The Cyanobacteria Hub!

If you've ever been curious about cyanobacteria, formerly known as "blue-green algae", then you've come to the right place!

On this website, you can find general information about cyanobacteria, as well as their diversification/phylogenetic history and their role in the environment. Additionally, you can read about harmful cyanobacteria blooms and how they effect the environment. Lastly, a photo gallery is featured on this website to demonstrate the beauty within these mighty microbes!

All information on this website is derived from scientific articles, which are listed in the References tab.
